Syntax: Inserts ➔ +Word Deletions ➔ -Word Unchanged ➔ =Word More ➔ "Word Word"

How to search

  • With the minus modifier you can search in removed parts: -shall
  • With the plus modifier you can search in added parts: +may
  • With the equal modifier you are searching for matches in both the original and the modified text: =unmodified
  • If you want to search for more than one word, you can enclose the words you are searching for with single oder double quotes: "The plags i am looking for" or 'The plags i am looking for'
  • If you combine search methods, only those documents matching them all will be found: +May -Shall
  • The asterisk (*) characted can be used as wildcard "The * i am looking for"
